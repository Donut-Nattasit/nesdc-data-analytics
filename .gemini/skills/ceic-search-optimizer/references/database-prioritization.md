# CEIC Database Prioritization Guide

To ensure high-quality and comparable data, follow this hierarchy when selecting series.

## 1. World Trend Plus (WTP) - Global Economic Monitor (GEM)
- **Use case**: Cross-country comparisons, regional analysis (ASEAN, G20), and standardized macroeconomic benchmarks.
- **Why**: GEM series are harmonized by CEIC experts to ensure methodological consistency across different countries.
- **Naming Pattern**: `[Metric]: [Sub-metric]: [Frequency]: [Country]` (e.g., `Real GDP: YoY: Quarterly: Thailand`).
- **Trigger**: Always search here first for GDP, CPI, and standard Unemployment data.

## 2. China Premium Database
- **Use case**: Deep-dive analysis into China's provincial data, specific industry sectors, or high-frequency national indicators.
- **Why**: Most comprehensive source for Chinese domestic data, often updated faster than global databases.
- **Trigger**: Use for "China Provincial GDP", "China Real Estate", or specific "China Industrial Production" series.

## 3. Global Database
- **Use case**: Standard national-level data when GEM is unavailable.
- **Why**: Contains the original series as reported by national source agencies.
- **Trigger**: Use for specialized national indicators (e.g., "Thailand Public Debt to GDP") or detailed balance of payments components.

## 4. Daily Database
- **Use case**: Financial markets, exchange rates, and commodity prices.
- **Trigger**: Use for "Gold Prices", "Stock Indices", or "Exchange Rates".
