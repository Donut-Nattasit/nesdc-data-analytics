# Report Structure of CPI Prediction

## Executive Summary

* Report predicted annual growth of CPI compared to most recent real data point
* Table shows annul growth predictions of CPI, CPI Core, CPI Energy, and CPI Raw Food compared to most recent real datareal data point
* Vertical bar chart of annul growth predictions of CPI, CPI Core, CPI Energy, and CPI Raw Food with 5-year historical data.

## CPI Overview

### CPI

* line chart of monthly CPI, CPI Core, CPI Energy, and CPI Raw Food (Historical data only) with data call out for the lastest data point
* line chart of YoY growth of monthly CPI, CPI Core, CPI Energy, and CPI Raw Food (Historical data only) with with data call out for the lastest data point

### CPI weight

* Pie chart with data lable of share of Core, Energy and Raw Food weight from 100% (use weight of the most recent month)
* Table showing weight by component, sorting from largest to smallest share. (use weight of the most recent month) Here is the columns of the table: ["Component", "Weight", "Group"] ("Group" are Core, Energy, or Raw Food)

### Contribution to Growth

* Stacked vertical bar chart: Contribution to Growth (YoY) by Core, Energy and Raw Food
* Stacked vertical bar chart: Contribution to Growth (YoY) by Component (select color carefully, make sure they are completely distinct)

## CPI Forecasting

### Monthly Forecast

* line chart of monthly prediction by component (Note: see component growth in `Appendix: CPI Growth by Component`)
* line chart of monthly prediction by composite: Headline, Core, Energy and Raw Food
* line chart of YoY growth of monthly composite forecast data starting from real data of the begining of current year to the end of forecasting horizon
* Table of YoY growth of monthly composite forecast data (Headline, Core, Energy and Raw Food) starting from real data of the begining of current year to the end of forecasting horizon

### Quarterly Forecast

* line chart of YoY growth of quarterly prediction by composite: Headline, Core, Energy and Raw Food (Note: see component growth in `Appendix: CPI Growth by Component`)
* Table of YoY growth of quarterly composite forecast data (Headline, Core, Energy and Raw Food) starting from real data of the begining of current year to the end of forecasting horizon

### Annual Forecast

* line chart of YoY growth of annual prediction by composite: Headline, Core, Energy and Raw Food (Note: see component growth in `Appendix: CPI Growth by Component`)
* Table of YoY growth of annual composite forecast data (Headline, Core, Energy and Raw Food) starting from real data of the begining of current year to the end of forecasting horizon
