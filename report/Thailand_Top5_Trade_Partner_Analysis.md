# Geographical Drivers of Trade: Thailand's Top 5 Exports Partner Analysis (2025–2026)

## Executive Summary
This macroeconomic report analyzes the specific country-level drivers behind the structural realignment of Thailand's top 5 export categories at the 2-digit Harmonized System (HS2) level. Drawing on granular bilateral records for the first five months (**January–May**) of **2025 and 2026**, we decompose the net USD shifts for each sector into their primary geographical **drivers** (growth contributors) and **drags** (growth detractors).

Our analysis isolates key country shifts that explain the divergent performance:
*   **Tech Surge (HS 85)** is heavily concentrated in the **U.S.A.**, which contributed a massive **+1.98 Billion USD** expansion, far eclipsing other markets.
*   **Automotive Collapse (HS 87)** is primarily driven by sharp contractions in regional ICE-consuming markets, particularly **Australia** and key ASEAN partners like the **Philippines**, indicating a severe cyclical slowdown.
*   **Rubber Crisis (HS 40)** is universally dragged down by **China**, showing a deep cooling in Chinese industrial demand for raw latex.
