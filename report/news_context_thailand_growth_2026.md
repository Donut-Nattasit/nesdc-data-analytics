# Thailand Economic Growth Context 2026

As of May 20, 2026, Thailand's economic growth is characterized by a resilient first-quarter performance followed by a steady, cautious projection for the remainder of the year.

### Key Highlights
* **Q1 2026 Growth:** The Thai economy grew by 2.8% in the first quarter of 2026, accelerating from 2.5% in Q4 2025.
* **Full-Year Forecast:** The National Economic and Social Development Council (NESDC) has maintained its full-year 2026 growth forecast at 1.5% to 2.5%.
* **Supporting Drivers:** Expansions in exports, manufacturing, private consumption, and public investment. A 400 billion-baht borrowing plan by the government aims to ease living costs and support clean energy transitions.
* **Challenges & Risks:**
  * Geopolitical tensions, notably the war in the Middle East impacting energy costs.
  * Downward adjustment in foreign tourist arrival expectations (lowered to 32 million from 35 million).
  * High levels of household debt remain a domestic concern.

*Sources: Channel News Asia, The Nation Thailand, The Edge Singapore, IMF.*
